What is CPI? How is inflation calculated?

The Consumer Price Index tracks changes in the cost of a representative “basket” of goods and services that urban consumers buy. Inflation is simply the percentage change in that index. So if CPI rises from 300 to 309, inflation is 3 percent.

CPI Data before 1912

CPI data from before the year 1912 is more speculatory than more recent years, due to it being a very long time ago. Although it's still accurate when you think about it.
